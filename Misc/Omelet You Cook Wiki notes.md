# other notes
- garnishes do come into contact with underlappers
- minerals do appear in basic bundles
- flaming can burn away mold
- unlocking ingredients = score x points from this ingredient
	- tin can + lemon, so the lemon makes the tin can worth pts
- wiggly ingredients can unstick themselves from sticky foods 
- mr kisser also applies to stuff that decreases xmult like jack o lantern
- ascetic trait also applies to ingredients earning $1 exactly
- sunny's daily request will show what tag he wants
	- ![[Omelet You Cook - Sunny's Daily Request.png]]
- spicy anti synergies with superstitious since you can't pick food up to put it out before it gets burnt 
- all duplicated helpers will keep their point/mult bonus (e.g. veggie/meat hater, cooler)
- all duplicated ingredients will give whatever is best for the player (avocado, dry aged salami)
- in catalogue, ingredient stats: number served, total scored points across all runs, best score with a single ingredient 
- atomically condensed milk will change description of ingredient once dispensed 
- if wannabe dj causes the omelet to come into contact with an ingredient on the side, it will "pick" it up 
- on superstitious, you can still click on wiggly ingredients to reset the timer
- Many Wiggly Ingredients have their own animation/hitbox thingies
	- Pufferfish expands
	- Crab gets up and walks around
	- Squid will do anticipation and nyoom
- bully customers can and will leave you in debt if they steal more than your earnings + savings 
	- best tag = tag that scored the most points 
- pointing ingredients will not count ingredients on the side
- truffle doesn't gain points from money earned from helpers (e.g. category collector)
