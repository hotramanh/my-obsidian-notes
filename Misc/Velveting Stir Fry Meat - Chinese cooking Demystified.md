1. SLICE MEAT
	- against the grain into ~3mm sheets
2. MARINATE WITH (per 400g) 
	 - Salt, 1/2 tsp 
	 - Sugar, 1 tsp 
	 - Shaoxing Wine, 1 tsp 
	 - ==Baking Soda, 1/2 tsp==
	 - Soy Sauce, 1/2 tsp 
	 - Dark Soy Sauce, 1/2 tsp 
	 - White Pepper, 1/4 tsp 
	 - ==Cornstarch, 4 tsp==
	 - ==1 Egg White==
	 - Oil, 1 Tbsp 
 3) PASS THROUGH OIL/WATER
	 -  Shallow fry/Poach at ~150c for ~30 seconds til done
 

- basking soda actually tenderizes
- cornstarch/egg white makes meat feel more tender in mouth
