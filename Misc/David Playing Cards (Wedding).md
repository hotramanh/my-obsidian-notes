# Color Palette
## Requirements
- Delphinium needs a shade of ==Cerulean Blue==
- Crocus Needs a color of ==purple== similar to the flower
- Edelweiss Needs to have ==white== as a major theme for its color
- All other flower (Nasturtium, Magnolia, Alstroemeria) can be any *colors or varieties found in the normal flowers* (or a more vibrant version of those natural colors)
