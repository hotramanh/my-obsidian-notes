==put into reader mode for quick calculations==

| Citrus     | Citrus Peel    | Citric Acid            | Malic Acid             | Water                    |
| ---------- | -------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **Lime**   | (lime:: 25)g   | `= this.lime * 0.66`g  | `= this.lime * 0.33`g  | `= this.lime * 16.66`g   |
| **Lemon**  | (lemon:: 22)g  | `= this.lemon * 1`g    | 0g                     | `= this.lemon * 16.66`g  |
| **Orange** | (orange:: 22)g | `= this.orange * 0.9`g | `= this.orange * 0.1`g | `= this.orange * 16.66`g |


1. Peel the citrus of choice, weigh the peels, and put them in a jar. Reserve the juice for later.
2. Add the appropriate amount of citric (and malic, for lime and orange) acid. 
3. Shake well and leave it for up to two hours. 
4. Place everything from the jar (including the peels) into a blender and add the water. 
5. Blend for half a minute.
6. Strain through a cheesecloth. 
7. Add the reserved juice of your citrus. 
8. Keep it in the fridge and use it within a week.
