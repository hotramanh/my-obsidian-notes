veggies:
- 1:1 carrot : daikon 
- 1:1 sugar : salt

brine: 
- 2:2:1 water : white vinegar : sugar
- 1 tsp per cup of water/vinegar

1) Combine brine ingredients and set to boil 
2) Julienne carrots/daikon 
3) Sprinkle sugar/salt into julienned veggies and knead for 3 min
4) Drain out excess water from veggies 
5) Put veggies then brine into jar
6) Wait for it to cool
7) Stick in the fridge for up to 2 weeks
